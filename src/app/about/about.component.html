Short bio about me.

My name is Diana Ruiz Garcia. I was born in Tucson, AZ, but I lived my childhood and teenage years in Mexico. My first language is Spanish, and I have been learning English since I came to live in the United States. In high school, we had to choose a specialty to prepare for college, and I decided on programming because, at that time, it caught my attention the most. Once I started taking programming classes, I began to like website development and learn more about technology. Once I finished my studies in high school, I wanted to continue with a career in computer systems at a university in Mexico, where I specialized in software. While I was finishing my studies, I had the opportunity to work in a software company where my main focus was the development of mobile applications in Android. Still, we had training every month, and I was able to learn languages such as Swift, Python, Angular, and NodeJS, among others. That's when I realized that I was passionate about web development and how useful it can be for application development.
